FAQ How do I start a Q&A?
1.) Click "Generate a new Q&A Tweet"
2.) Type the Tweet
3.) Click Generate Tweet
4.) Copy the tweet and tweet it from the twitter app.

How do I get videos from my existing Q&A?
1.) Choose "View videos from the last Q&A tweet"
2.) Add videos that you want to emailed by clicking "Add to List"
3.) Click "Email me the videos!"
4.) Enter your email and press send
5.) The links to those videos will be emailed to you so you can add the actual video question in your youtube Q&A video.
